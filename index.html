FAQ

Frequently asked.

Everything you might want to know before reaching out.

1. How does ReverseWorldGo's reverse translation work?

ReverseWorldGo analyzes your text using on-device AI to generate reversed, mirrored, and upside-down versions in multiple writing styles. All processing happens on your device — nothing is uploaded to remote servers.

2. How do I restore my subscription?

Open ReverseWorldGo and go to Profile → Upgrade to Premium → Restore Purchases. You can also manage subscriptions at iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → ReverseWorldGo Premium. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

3. Where is my data stored? Is it private?

All journal entries, mirror photos, voice memos, and reverse translations are stored locally on your device. We do not upload any personal data to our servers, and your content is never used to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

4. What iOS versions and devices are supported?

ReverseWorldGo requires iOS 17.0 or later and runs natively on both iPhone and iPad. Tested on iPhone 13 and newer, all iPad Pro models (M1+), and iPad Air (M2+). Universal binary — one purchase works on all your devices.

5. How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → ReverseWorldGo Premium → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep Premium access until then. After cancellation, the app falls back to the free tier automatically.

6. Can I use ReverseWorldGo offline?

Yes — all core features (reverse translation, voice journaling, mirror camera filters, parallel explorer) work fully offline. AI-powered translation caches results locally so repeat lookups are instant with no network required.
