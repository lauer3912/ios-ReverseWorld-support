Manage Your Subscription

Premium subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To view, change, or cancel your subscription:

iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → ReverseWorldGo Premium

You can also restore purchases inside the app via Profile → Upgrade to Premium → Restore Purchases.
